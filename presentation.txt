⏱️ Remote Pitch Script: Friction V2.0 — The Anti-Streak Recovery App
0:00 – 0:30 | The Hook & The Problem
"Addiction is a frictionless slide. Every app on your phone is engineered to remove the barrier between impulse and action—through one-tap buying, auto-play, and infinite scroll. To fight this, traditional recovery apps use gamified streak counters. But behavioral psychology reveals a dark truth: when a user breaks a 47-day streak, they experience intense shame. This 'shame spiral' actually triggers a heavier binge and causes them to abandon the app entirely. The tool meant to save them becomes the source of trauma."

0:30 – 1:15 | The Solution & Screen-Share Walkthrough
"We built Friction—the only app designed to add resistance back. As you can see on my screen, it is a zero-barrier Progressive Web App that acts as an immediate emergency brake. When an urge hits, the user enters an adaptive 4-Gate pipeline. First, they confront 'Time Theft' via this tactile cost-benefit slider. Next, they face an upgraded Stroop Effect engine—notice how the button locations randomly shuffle on every single round to completely shatter muscle memory and force cognitive focus."

1:15 – 1:45 | Remote Tech Showcase
"If a user is too panicked to complete a gate, an automated 'Escape Hatch' instantly steps in, bypassing the complex tasks and guiding them straight to a tremor-forgiving breathing switch. Crucially, we destroyed the streak. In its place is the Resilience Ledger, tracking Interventions Survived instead of days clean—rewarding effort over perfection. Built entirely client-side with persistent IndexedDB storage, it is completely private, fully offline-capable, and immune to OS cache sweeps."

1:45 – 2:00 | The Close & Call to Action
"Friction works because it doesn't track the addiction; it interrupts the impulse. Addiction thrives on frictionless design. Friction is the handrail. I've dropped the live deployment link directly into the meeting chat—I invite you to open it and test the resistance yourself. Thank you."
